FAQ

Sawaal jo sabke mann mein hain.

Kya app offline kaam karta hai? +

Haan — ArvoLens offline-first hai. Manual entry, basic reports, PDF export — sab internet ke bina kaam karta hai. Sirf AI features (receipt scan, voice parsing) ke liye internet chahiye. Offline entries automatically sync ho jaati hain jab internet aata hai.

Kya SMS permissions leni padti hain? +

Nahi! ArvoLens SMS access nahi karta — sirf notification access use karta hai. Ye Play Store ke guidelines ke according hai aur tumhara data zyada safe rehta hai. Bank notifications se automatically transactions detect hoti hain.

Sirf UPI track hoga ya baaki payments bhi? +

Sab kuch track hota hai — UPI, NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, credit card, debit card, bank transfer — koi bhi payment jiske liye bank notification aati hai, ArvoLens automatically detect karega. Cash ke liye voice ya manual entry available hai.

CA-ready report mein exactly kya hota hai? +

CA-ready report mein category-wise expense breakdown, payment method summary (UPI vs cash vs card), GST amounts (jahan applicable ho), merchant names, dates — sab organized format mein hota hai. PDF ya Excel mein export karo aur directly CA ko WhatsApp pe bhejo.

Lifetime plan kab tak available rahega? +

Lifetime plan sirf launch offer hai — limited time ke liye available hai. App launch ke baad ye plan hata liya jaayega ya price badh jaayegi. Jo log waitlist mein hain unhe pehle access milega aur launch offer ka fayda milega.

Android aur iOS dono pe available hai? +

Haan — ArvoLens Flutter pe bana hai toh Android aur iOS dono pe available hoga. Android pe auto-detection feature full support karega. iOS pe scan, voice, manual entry — sab kaam karega. Auto-detection iOS pe partially available hoga Apple ke restrictions ki wajah se.

Mera data safe hai? +

Bilkul. Sab data encrypted hai. Biometric lock available hai app pe. Data kabhi third party ko nahi becha jaata. Local storage by default — cloud backup optional hai (Premium mein). Tumhara data sirf tumhara.
